How do I find a professional near me?

You can use the search functionality on our website to find professionals based on your location. Simply enter your location or enable location services on your device to find professionals nearby.

Can I book a professional for a specific date and time?

Yes, you can specify your preferred date and time when searching for a professional. The search results will show available professionals who match your criteria.

How do I register as a user?

To register as a user, click on the "User Registration" option in the navigation bar. Fill out the required information, including your name, phone number, Aadhar number, address, and skills.

Can I leave a review for a professional?

Yes, after receiving services from a professional, you can leave a review and rating based on your experience. Navigate to the professional's profile and find the review section to leave your feedback.

How do I reset my password?

If you forget your password, you can click on the "Forgot Password" option on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

How can I contact a professional directly?

You can contact a professional directly through their profile page. Look for their contact information, such as phone number or email, and reach out to them for further inquiries or to schedule an appointment.

Are the professionals on your platform verified?

Yes, we verify the credentials and qualifications of all professionals before listing them on our platform. This ensures that you receive services from reliable and skilled individuals.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, log in to your account and navigate to the profile settings. From there, you can edit your personal details, contact information, and skills.

Do I need to pay for using your platform?

No, our platform is free for users to search for professionals and use other features such as registration, booking, and leaving reviews. However, you may need to pay the professional directly for their services.

Can I hire a professional for multiple days?

Yes, you can hire a professional for multiple days if needed. When booking a professional, specify the duration of the job, and they will schedule accordingly.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

If you're not satisfied with the service provided by a professional, please contact us immediately. We will work with you and the professional to resolve any issues and ensure your satisfaction.
